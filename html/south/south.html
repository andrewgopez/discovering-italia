Southern Italy (Italia Meridionale)

Southern Italy (Italia Meridionale), often called the Mezzogiorno, is located in the lower part of the Italian peninsula. With a warmer Mediterranean climate, dramatic coastlines, mountains, and fertile lands, the region has been influenced by many civilizations, including the Greeks, Romans, Byzantines, Normans, and Spanish. Southern Italy is celebrated for its ancient history, strong cultural identity, traditional music, and distinctive cuisine.
