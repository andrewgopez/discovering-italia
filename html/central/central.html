Central Italy (Italia Centrale)

Central Italy (Italia Centrale) is the cultural heart of the country, home to many of Italy’s most famous historical and artistic treasures. The area includes rolling hills, the Apennine Mountains, and coastal landscapes along the Tyrrhenian and Adriatic Seas. Known as the birthplace of the Renaissance, Central Italy has played a major role in shaping Italian art, literature, politics, and traditions.
