Northern Italy (Italia Settentrionale)

Located at the top of the Italian peninsula, Northern Italy (Italia Settentrionale) is known for its Alpine landscapes, large lakes, and highly developed cities. The region has been shaped by influences from neighboring European countries such as France, Switzerland, Austria, and Slovenia. It is famous for its industrial centers, historic cities, world-class art, and diverse cuisine ranging from mountain traditions to Mediterranean flavors.
